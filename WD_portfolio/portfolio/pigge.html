PIGGE is a single player stealth game that portrays the dictatorship and oppression felt in Portugal during Estado-Novo. You play as a young art student, Filip Fox, who wants to leave his revolutionary mark in the city, hoping to inspire many to claim their deserved freedom.

PIGGE was initially idealized by 3 students from ETIC studying Animation and Videogames, whose main focus is art. They contacted me to help make their game a reality and I took part of the development team as the only programmer and a game designer.

Roles in the development:

  • Programming - The game was written in C# using Unity.
  • Game Design - I aided the lead designer with all game design choices.

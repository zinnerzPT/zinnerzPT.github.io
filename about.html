Hello, I am Francisco and I'm a game developer and programmer from Portugal.

I started playing videogames at the age of 5 when I got my first Game Boy Color with a copy of Pokémon Gold, and I've been fascinated by videogames ever since. I continued playing videogames throughout my childhood on the Playstation 2 and PC, and eventually found my way to collectable card games as well, starting with Yu-Gi-Oh! when I was only 9 and figuring it out on the school playground and eventually Magic The Gathering when I was 15.

When the time came for me to pick a degree, I decided that I wanted to develop games so, at the time, I picked Computer Science which was the closest and best option for me to be able to pursue game development. Later on, after 3 years, I decided to drop out, as a new course was made available, one that was fully focused on making games. I then enrolled in the Videogames & Multimedia bachelor's degree.

I completed my bachelor's degree of Videogames & Multimedia in 2021 and I have been professionally working as a Unity developer since.

I started my professional life through a four month internship with the porting team at Nerd Monkeys where I dabbled into developing for the Nintendo Switch, having been credited on SGC - Short Games Collection.

I worked, as part of a two person development team, on Capelo e Ivens - Explorando Africa as part of a partnership between Redcatpig and Câmara Municipal de Palmela. I worked on all the programming and Unity integration, along with sharing the Game Design workload with the other team member.

As a member of Redcatpig, I worked on several other projects. I worked as a gameplay programmer on Cryptopolis for a little over a year, where I was in charge of a lot of the item, inventory and furniture placement systems.

I was the lead developer on a 360 degree video player - Dayholi - for the JioDive VR platform
